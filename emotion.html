Loving-kindness

Cultivation of Mettā, i.e. benevolence, kindness and amity. Silent repetitions of phrases like “may you be happy” directed at a person who, depending on tradition, may or may not be internally visualized.

Loving kindness study

(Lutz et al 2008)

  • Group 1: Expert Meditators, Group 2: Novices
  • Brain scan while practicing meditation.
  • Question: Does loving-kindness meditation change the brain?

Metta increases activity in brain regions involved in emotion and empathy for others.

Loving kindness and PTSD

(Kearney et al 2013)

  • Group 1: Veterans w/ PTSD, Group 2: Control
  • 12-week loving kindness meditation class
  • Question: Does loving-kindness meditation change the brain?

Reduced PTSD symptoms 3 months after course.

Practice loving-kindness meditation for:

  • Improved mood
  • More altruistic behavior
  • Reduced stress
  • Reduced PTSD symptoms
