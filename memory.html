Mindfulness and Learning Study

Mrazek et al, 2013

  • Group 1: 2-week mindfulness class, Group 2: Nutrition class
  • Question: Can mindfulness improve academic performance?

Better GRE score and better working memory.

Mindfulness Memory Study

Brown et al, 2016

Question: Does mindfulness-based meditation improve recognition and recall?

  • Higher mindfulness scores (no training) related to better memory.
  • Brief training in focused attention form of mindfulness training improves recognition and free recall.

Mindfulness Induction Study (Wilson et al 2015)

  • 150 undergraduates
  • Assigned to 15 minutes of mindfulness vs 15 minutes of mind-wandering
  • "Because mindfulness meditation encourages judgment-free thoughts and feelings, we predicted that participants in the mindfulness condition would be especially likely to form false memories."

Practice mindfulness meditation for:

  • Better academic performance
  • Better memory
  • But beware: higher suceptibility for false memories?
